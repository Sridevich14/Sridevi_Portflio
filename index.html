About me


Hello, I'm Sridevi, a frontend developer dedicated to crafting immersive digital experiences. With a passion for clean code and pixel-perfect design, I bring creativity and technical expertise to every project.

My journey into web development started with a curiosity about how websites were built. I delved into HTML and CSS, fascinated by their ability to transform ideas into interactive interfaces. As I explored JavaScript and frontend frameworks like React.js, I realized the immense potential to create dynamic, user-friendly applications.


Currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering, I have gained a strong foundation in programming languages, data structures, algorithms, and software development methodologies. My coursework at VIT Vellore has equipped me with the knowledge and skills to tackle complex problems and contribute meaningfully to the tech industry.
